Our Story & Mission

Who We Are

HITLUR is a premium wholesale clothing company operating across six locations in three major cities. We specialize in connecting quality manufacturers with retail businesses, providing curated fashion collections at competitive wholesale prices.

Founded with a vision to modernize wholesale fashion distribution, we serve over 420 dedicated staff members and thousands of retail partners. Our cloud-powered CRM and warehouse management systems ensure seamless operations across all our locations.

We believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust, quality, and reliability. Every collection we curate goes through rigorous quality checks to ensure our retail partners always receive the best products for their customers.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide the highest quality wholesale fashion while building lasting business partnerships across Central Asia and Europe.

👁 Our Vision

To become the leading wholesale fashion platform connecting manufacturers and retailers globally.

💎 Quality First

Every product in our catalogue is carefully selected and quality-checked before reaching our partners.

🤝 Partnership

We grow when our partners grow. Long-term relationships are at the core of everything we do.
